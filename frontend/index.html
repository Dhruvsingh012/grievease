How It Works

From submission to resolution in 4 steps.

1️⃣ Student Submits

Student logs in and describes the issue. AI predicts category and priority. Duplicate detection prevents repeat submissions.

2️⃣ Auto-Assigned

System instantly routes the complaint to the correct department staff. SLA clock starts immediately.

3️⃣ Staff Resolves

Staff updates status, adds internal notes, communicates via comments. Every step logged in the timeline.

4️⃣ Student Rates

On resolution, student rates the experience 1–5 stars. Average rating feeds the analytics dashboard.

Student Staff Admin Features FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a complaint?

Login with your name, email and admission number. Fill the complaint form — AI will auto-predict the category. Submit and track in real-time.

Can I edit my complaint?

Yes — you can edit the title and description as long as the complaint is in Pending or Assigned status.

Who handles my complaint?

Complaints are auto-routed to the relevant department — IT complaints to IT Team, Hostel complaints to Hostel Management, and so on.

What if my complaint is not resolved?

The escalation engine automatically escalates it to the Department Head, then Admin, then Principal at defined time intervals.

How does AI prediction work?

Your complaint text is vectorized using TF-IDF and classified by a Logistic Regression model trained on 1300 labeled grievances across 10 categories.

Is my data secure?

All sessions use JWT authentication. Passwords are bcrypt-hashed. Internal staff notes are hidden from students completely.
